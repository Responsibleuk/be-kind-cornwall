Kind Cornwall is a community for Cornish Lifestyle Businesses. Meet like-minded and diverse people tackling the issues they care about. Listen to our show, get involved with events, find out how you can contribute, and discover other lifestyle businesses.

About Kind Cornwall

Small businesses are the foundation of strong communities.

They bring energy, personality and diversity to the communities they serve but running a lifestyle business can be lonely … doing everything by yourself … no team to share the load.

Kind Cornwall is a new social enterprise bringing together and supporting the best lifestyle businesses in the beautiful county of Cornwall.
